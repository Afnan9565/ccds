School Handbook

The school handbook should be brought to school daily for the entire session.

The School handbook as a medium of communication between the school and parents records the activities, performances, classroom behavrious, discipline and progress of your child.

All the record of non-attendance and late coming should be entered in the proper assigned pages of the handbook.

Any student damaging the handbook or an others losing the handbook will be subject to disciplinary action.

Parents shall send written application, incase of handbook damage or loss, requesting the issue of new handbook. In such cases a fine of Rs. 100/- will be levied and can be purchased paying its amount with necessary remarks about its loss and date of re-issue, duly signed by the incharge.
